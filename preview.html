About Me

Welcome!

I'm David Dickson, an undergraduate at Harvard studying Computer Science and Statistics on the Machine Learning track, with a language citation in Mandarin Chinese. You can contact me by email at dddickson@college.harvard.edu or find me on LinkedIn — I'd love to hear from you!

Outside of academics, I'm involved in a lot — from mentoring students at the Harvard Ed Portal, to helping run Asia's largest student-run conference through HAUSCR, to coordinating company visits for Black STEM students through HSBSE. I'm also a dancer (Harvard Eleganza), a self-proclaimed singer, an avid cookie baker, and a lover of manga.

This site is a personal corner of the internet — part introduction, part time capsule.
